Our History

From humble beginnings to a premier institution

Foundation

1985

SRM University was founded with a vision to provide quality education in engineering and technology. Started with just 3 departments and 180 students.

Deemed University Status

2002

Recognized as a Deemed-to-be-University under Section 3 of UGC Act, marking a significant milestone in our academic journey.

NAAC A+ Accreditation

2010

Awarded A+ grade by NAAC, recognizing our commitment to academic excellence, research, and infrastructure development.

Research Excellence

2018

Established 15 research centers and collaborated with top global universities for joint research programs.

NIRF Top 50

2026

Ranked among top 50 universities in India by NIRF. Campus expanded to 250+ acres with 10,000+ students.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in higher education, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development to create responsible citizens and future-ready professionals.

Our Mission

Provide quality education through innovative teaching, promote cutting-edge research, build industry partnerships, and nurture entrepreneurial spirit among students.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, Inclusivity, Sustainability, and Social Responsibility guide everything we do at SRM.

Admission Process

Simple and transparent admission process

1

Fill Application

Complete the online application form with personal and academic details.

2

Upload Documents

Upload required documents including mark sheets, certificates, and photo.

3

Application Review

Our admission committee reviews your application and documents.

4

Confirm Admission

Receive offer letter, pay fees, and confirm your seat at SRM.

Eligibility Criteria

UG Programs: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks (PCM for B.Tech)

PG Programs: Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks

MBA: Any graduate with valid CAT/MAT/XAT score

MCA: BCA/B.Sc IT with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation

Required Documents

• 10th & 12th Mark Sheets

• Degree Certificate (for PG applicants)

• Transfer Certificate

• Conduct Certificate

• Passport Size Photographs

• ID Proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)

Fee Structure

B.Tech: INR 1,50,000 per year

BBA/BCA/B.Com: INR 80,000 per year

M.Tech: INR 1,20,000 per year

MBA: INR 2,00,000 per year

MCA: INR 1,00,000 per year

Scholarships

Merit Scholarship: Up to 100% tuition fee waiver for top rankers

Sports Scholarship: Up to 50% for national-level athletes

Need-based Aid: Financial assistance for deserving students

Research Fellowship: For MTech/PhD students with stipend
